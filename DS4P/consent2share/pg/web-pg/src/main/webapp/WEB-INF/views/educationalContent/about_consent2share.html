Using Consent2Share

The Consent 2 Share system can help you make decisions on who can look at and receive your health information. You are in control. When you create your consent policy, the C2S system protects your information from being shared to unauthorized providers. C2S shares information like your diagnosis, medications and medical history. But, C2S does not share the content of conversations you have or thoughts you share with your doctors or therapists.

Consent 2 Share (C2S) is a system that gives you the option to share all or part of your health record with your doctors. Your doctor or provider can access your health information and make better decisions on how to treat your medical condition. With this revolutionary technology, C2S can help you:

  • Define which providers you want to share your medical records with.
  • Define your privacy policy and sign an electronic consent.
  • Compare or review your privacy policy against your actual health record, showing you what information you agree to share or do not want to share.
  • Receive and send messages to your doctor.
  • Delegate management of your privacy and consent to a caregiver.

Sharing your information can also benefit you!

Sharing your medical information can also help improve your health when shared:

  • To connect all of your doctors during your treatment
  • To make sure doctors have all of your medical information so you can receive better care
  • To coordinate all of your health care
  • To pay doctors and hospitals for your treatment
  • To protect the public's health, such as by reporting when the flu is in your area
