Electronic health information exchange (HIE) allows doctors, nurses, pharmacists, and other health care providers to have access to your vital medical information electronically through a computer system. This way, your doctors can get important medical information about you quickly. Instead of your medical information stored on paper in a filing cabinet at various doctor's offices, the electronic sharing of information can greatly improve the completeness of your medical record information and help you get better care.

Informing Your Doctor

Inform your doctors and therapists that you will be sharing with them health information from other heath care providers. They will feel connected to every health care provider who treats you, and can care for you better.

Health Information Privacy

Here is a list of the types of health information that is protected and cannot be shared without your consent:

  • Information that your doctors, therapists, nurses, and other health care providers put in your medical record
  • Conversations your doctor has about your care or treatment with nurses and others
  • Information about you in your health insurer's computer system
  • Billing information about you at your clinic
  • Most other health information about you held by those who must follow these laws
